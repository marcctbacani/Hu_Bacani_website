Hu & Bacani was founded simply on the joy of making cool stuff happen. Marc Bacani and Dounan Hu joined forces after many long conversations about amazing things that can be done by combinating business acumen, tech ingenuity, and design thinking. The partnership brings together 15+ years of combined experience in digital marketing, tech, and entrepreneurship.

 

Originally from Shanghai, Dounan grew up in the U.S. and Canada, teaching himself how to code from age 13. His love of tech stems from finding solutions for building innovative products. He studied astronomy and physics at Cornell and Cambridge. He has worked with and consulted for many tech startups in the U.S. and China including Wikipedia, Aihehuo, and The Bynar group. One of his greatest passions is building tech solutions for social enterprises - some of his work include tech solutions for GiveGab.com and Feiy.co, and companies under the Acumen Fund.

 

Marc is a native of Houston and did his bachelors at Austin College in Political Science and Communications. After Texas, he wanted to better understand business on a global scale and studied at Hult International Business School in Shanghai. Interested in innovative marketing from a young age, his passion is in finding a human touch to branding while focusing on core business objectives through a love of analytics. He has worked for and done marketing consultation for companies in Shanghai, Hong Kong, and San Francisco including; Intel, Ozmo, and Avibe.

 

The partnership started through the Le Wagon community in Shanghai, where both saw a need for digital business and marketing solutions for young companies. Companies that need to provide seamless, beautiful, and conversion-driven journeys for their users.
